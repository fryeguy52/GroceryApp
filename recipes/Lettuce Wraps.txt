##Recipe

1 lb. fat-free lean ground turkey
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
4 green onions, thinly sliced
1 (8 oz) can sliced water chestnuts, drained and coarsely chopped
large handful baby carrots, chopped
1 package sliced mushrooms, chopped
2 Tbsp lower-sodium soy sauce (green top)
1 Tbsp rice vinegar
12 Boston lettuce leaves
2 cucumbers


- Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add turkey, mushrooms, carrots, water chestnuts, garlic and ginger to the pan and cook for about 6 minutes or until turkey is browned. Add a little soy sauce to taste.  Add green onions.
- Peel both cucumbers, dice 1/2 and slice 1 1/2.  Put diced cucumber in turkey mixture.  Mix together rice vinegar, sugar, sesame seeds, and red pepper flakes.  Pour dressing over sliced cucumber.
- Serve in lettuce leaves.  Garnish with peanuts and peanut sauce.
