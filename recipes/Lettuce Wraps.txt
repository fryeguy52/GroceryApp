##Recipe

2 lb. fat-free lean ground turkey
2 Tbsp olive oil
4 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
8 green onions, thinly sliced
1 (8 oz) can sliced water chestnuts, drained and coarsely chopped
large handful baby carrots, chopped
2 package sliced mushrooms, chopped
4 Tbsp lower-sodium soy sauce (green top)
2 Tbsp rice vinegar
12 Boston lettuce leaves
1 cucumber
1 handful peanuts
1 teaspoon sesame seeds
1 teaspoon sugar
.25 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 bag edamame

- Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add turkey, mushrooms, carrots, water chestnuts, garlic and ginger to the pan and cook for about 10 minutes or until turkey is browned. Add soy sauce to taste.
- Peel and dice 1 cucumbers and green onions.  Mix into in turkey mixture.  
- Boil water, add frozen edamame, remove from heat.
- Serve in lettuce leaves.  Garnish with peanuts and peanut sauce.
