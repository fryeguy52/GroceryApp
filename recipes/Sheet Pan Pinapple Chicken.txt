##recipe
Ingredients
===========

- 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 3 bell peppers
- 1 red onion
- 20oz chunk pineapple in juice
- 1/4 cup pineapple juice
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tsp sesame oil
- 2 cups rice
- 1 bag edamame

Directions
===========
1. Preheat oven to 350F.

1. Open the pineapple and drain; reserve 1/4 cup of juice for the sauce and set the pineapple chunks to the side.

1. In a small mixing bowl combine pineapple juice, soy sauce, and sesame oil.

1. Cube the chicken breast, onion, and bell peppers and place in a large mixing bowl with the pineapple chunks.

1. Pour sauce over the chicken, bell peppers and pineapples and toss to coat.

1. Spread in an even layer on the baking sheet.

1. Place baking sheet in the oven and cook for 15 minutes at 350F.

1. Start rice.

1. Remove from oven, toss, and cook for an additional 15 minutes.

1. Heat edamame.

1. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and allow to cool slightly.

1. Enjoy.
