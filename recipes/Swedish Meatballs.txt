##Recipe
1 pound ground beef
.5 cup bread crumbs
1 package swedish meatball mix
2 cups milk
1 bag egg noodles
2 cans green beans

Mix beef, bread crumbs, meatball mix, and a little water in a bowl.  Roll into small meatballs.  Cook in pan.  Make sauce with mix and milk.
Cook egg noodles to package direction.
Warm beans.
