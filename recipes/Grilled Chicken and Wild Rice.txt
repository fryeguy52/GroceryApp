##Recipe
3 chicken breasts
3 cups wild rice
2 zucchini
2 pounds mini bell pepper
1 tablespoon olive oil


- start rice in rice cooker
- cut chicken in half (thickness). season with garlic, salt, pepper
- cut zucchini in 1/4s
- grill chicken and veggies
