##Recipe
chicken thighs
bread
.5 box pasta (rotini or farfalle)
14 oz can artichoke hearts in brine, quartered
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
1 English cucumber, diced
.5 red onion, sliced
.5 jar kalamata olives, halved
.5 can garbanzo beans
crumbled feta
1 tsp Dijon mustard
3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp dried thyme
1.5 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp dried basil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 pepper
1 tsp salt
3 tbsp vinegar (Balsamic or red wine)
6 tbsp olive oil


- Marinate chicken with 2 T olive oil, 2 cloves garlic, .5 tsp oregano, and 1 T lemon juice.  Grill.
- Cook pasta according to package directions.
- Chop veggies.
- Mix remaining ingredients into dressing; combine with pasta and veggies.
- Serve with bread.
