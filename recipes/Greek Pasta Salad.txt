##Recipe
chicken thighs
bread
.5 box pasta (rotini or farfalle)
14 oz can artichoke hearts in brine, quartered
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
1 English cucumber, diced
.5 red onion, sliced
.5 jar kalamata olives, halved
1 can garbanzo beans
crumbled feta
1 bottle Dion's Greek
1 tin dolmas


- Marinate chicken.  Grill.
- Cook pasta according to package directions.
- Chop veggies.
- Combine chicken, pasta, veggies, and dressing.
- Serve with dolmas.
