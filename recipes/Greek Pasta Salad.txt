##Recipe
4 chicken breasts
bread
1 box pasta (rotini or farfalle)
14 oz can artichoke hearts in brine, quartered
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
1 English cucumber, diced
1 red onion, sliced
1 jar black olives
1 block feta, crumbled
1 tsp Dijon mustard
2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp dried basil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pepper
1 tsp salt
3 tbsp vinegar (Balsamic or red wine)
4 tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped


- Grill chicken.
- Cook pasta according to package directions.
- Chop veggies.
- Mix remaining ingredients into dressing; combine with pasta and veggies.
- Garnish with parsley.
- Serve with bread.
