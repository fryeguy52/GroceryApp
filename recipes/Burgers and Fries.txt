##Recipe
1 pound ground beef | frozen patties
1 head lettuce
2 large tomatos
4 large potatoes
1 jar pickles
1 onions
8 hamburger buns
1 bottle ketchup
1 tablespoon Mrs. Dash
1 pack sliced cheese

* cut potatoes into wedges
* cover potatoes with olive oil and mrs dash, bake at 425 in pamed cookie sheet for ~25 min
* slice tomato, onions, pickles
* cook meat on the grill
