##Ingredients
1, , beef pot roast
1, package, mushrooms
1, , mushroom soup mix
1, head, cauliflower
2, cups, carrots
2, lbs, small white potatoes
##Recipe
* chop all the vegitables
* put veggies in the crockpot on the bottom
* put post roast on top of the veggies fat side up
* if using soup mix: put dry mix on top of roast, add ~1 cup of water to veggies
  if using canned soup: pour over roast
* cook on high for 6-8 hours
