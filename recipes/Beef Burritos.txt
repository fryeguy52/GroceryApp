##Recipe
1 pound ground beef
3 large potatoes
1 onion
2 cloves fresh garlic
4, oz green chile
3 cups rice
1 can black beans
2 avocado
8 flour tortillas
8 oz mexican blend cheese
2 medium tomatoes
.25 teaspoon cumin
1 tablespoon olive oil

Potatoes
* preheat oven to 425
* chop potatoes into 1/2 cubes
* coat potatoes in olive oil, salt, and pepper and place on pamed baking sheet.
* bake on 425 until done

Rice
* make the rice in the rice maker

Meat
* dice onion put in pan with all the meat
* brown meat on the stove season with garlic, cumin, and green chile

Other
*chop tomatos and avocados
