##Recipe
1 pound ground beef
3 large potatoes
1 onion
2 cloves fresh garlic
4, oz green chile
3 cups rice
1 can black beans
2 avocado
8 flour tortillas
8 oz mexican blend cheese
2 medium tomatoes
.25 teaspoon cumin
1 tablespoon olive oil


- Preheat oven to 425.  Chop potatoes into 1/2 inch cubes.  Coat potatoes in olive oil, salt, and pepper and place on pamed baking sheet.  Bake on 425 until done.
- Cook rice in the rice maker.
- Dice onion put in pan with all the meat.  Brown meat on the stove season with garlic, cumin, and green chile.
- Chop tomatos and avocados.
