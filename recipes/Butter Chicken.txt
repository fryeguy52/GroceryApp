##recipe
1 yellow onion, diced
1/3 jar butter chicken sauce
2 lbs chicken, diced
1 can diced tomatoes, drained
.5 cup whipping cream
4 tbsp butter or ghee
3 cups rice
5 heads broccoli 
2 pieces naan
1 box samosas

Instructions
- Start rice in rice cooker.
- Turn on oven.  Once preheated, bake samosas per directions.
- Add a small amount of oil to large fryer pan; sautee onions until golden.
- Add butter chicken paste and 1/4 c water and cook until water evaporates.
- Add chicken to pan and brown.
- Add diced tomatoes and 1/2 c water to pan.  Saute until chicken is cooked through.
- Mix in 1/4 c butter and 1/2 c cream.
- Heat naan in warmed oven.
- Serve and enjoy!
