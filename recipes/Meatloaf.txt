##recipe
2 pounds ground beef
1 cup crushed ritz crackers
1 cup milk
.5 teaspoon pepper
.5 cup chopped onion
.5 cup ketchup
1 egg
1 teaspoon salt
.5 teaspoon garlic powder
6 potatoes
sour cream
milk
butter
1 can green beans

Preheat oven to 350.  Mix meatloaf ingredients (trhough garlic powder) together.  Form into two smaller loaves and put on cookie sheet.  Bake for 45 minutes or until done.  Boil potatoes until fork tender.  Drain, mix in milk, butter, and sour cream to taste.  Heat green beans.
