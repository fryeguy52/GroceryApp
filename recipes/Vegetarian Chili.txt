## recipe
2 cans (15 ounces each) pinto beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
1 can (16 ounces) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15-1/2 ounces) hominy, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 ounces) garbanzo beans or chickpeas, rinsed and drained
1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chilies, undrained
2 small zucchini, halved and thinly sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
1-1/2 to 2 cups water
1 to 2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
0.5 teaspoon sugar
2 boxes cornbread mix
3 eggs
0.66 c milk
0.25 c greek yogurt or sour cream
0.5 cup shredded Monterey Jack 




- In a Dutch oven, combine the chili ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30-35 minutes or until vegetables are tender. 
- Make cornbread according to package directions, adding an extra egg and 0.25 cup greek yogurt.
- Garnish chili with cheese.
