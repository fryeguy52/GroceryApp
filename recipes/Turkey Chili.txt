##recipe
2 lb of ground turkey
2 28 oz cans of tomato sauce
2 cans of black beans, drained and rinsed
2 cans of pinto beans, drained and rinsed
2 can of petite diced tomatoes, undrained
2 cans of corn
4 large cloves of garlic, minced
2 T paprika
2 T chili powder
4 t ground cumin
3 t ground oregano
1 bag shredded cheese
1 bag fritos


- Put everything but fritos in crockpot.  Cook on low for 8-10 hours.  
- Serve with shredded cheese and fritos.
