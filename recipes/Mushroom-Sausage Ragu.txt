##recipe
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 sweet Italian sausages
2 containers mixed mushrooms, sliced 
Salt and freshly ground pepper
2 thinly sliced shallots
1 tablespoon tomato paste
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1 pound pasta
Chopped parsley


- In a skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of the oil.
- Add the sausages, cover and cook over moderate heat until no longer pink within; slice 1/4 inch thick.
- Heat the remaining oil in the skillet. Add the mushrooms, season with salt and pepper and cook until softened.
- Add the shallots; cover and cook for 4 minutes.
- Add 0.5 cup water or broth.
- Add the sausages and simmer for 2 minutes. Stir in the tomato paste. Season with salt and pepper.
- Off the heat, swirl in the butter and sprinkle with parsley.
- Serve over pasta.
