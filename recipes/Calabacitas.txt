##Recipe
2 zucchini
2 tomatoes
1 onion
1 can corn, drained and rinsed
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
green chile
shredded cheddar cheese
3 cups rice
2 avocados


- Cook rice in IP.
- Heat oil in jumbo fryer over medium/medium-high heat.  Dice zucchini, onion, and tomatoes.  
- Saute onion and zucchini until nearly cooked.  
- Add tomatoes, corn, black beans, and chile.  Cook until heated through.
- Add cheese (approx 1 c?).  Heat until cheese is melted, stirring to combine.
- Serve over rice with sliced avocado and additional cheese.
