##Recipe
4, ,pork chops
1, stick, butter
4, oz, sour cream
6, large, potatoes
1 head, garlic


- cut garlic in half, coat in olive oil and wrap in foil
- chop and boil potatoes
- grill pork and garlic
- mix butter, sour cream and roasted garlic into potatoes
