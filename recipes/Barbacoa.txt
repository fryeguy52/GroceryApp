##recipe
Adobo Sauce
 - 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
 - 3 -4 chipotle chiles in adobo
 - 4 teaspoons minced garlic cloves
 - 4 teaspoons cumin
 - 2 teaspoons oregano
 - 3 tablespoons lime juice
 - 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
3 whole bay leaves
4 -5 lbs chuck roast
3/4 cup chicken broth
rice
beans
tortillas
tomatoes
avocados


- Mix adobo sauce in food processor.  
- Add chuck roast to crockpot.  Cover with sauce, bay leaves, and broth.  
- Cook on low for 8-10 hours.  
- Cook rice, heat beans.  Chop garnish.  Serve.
