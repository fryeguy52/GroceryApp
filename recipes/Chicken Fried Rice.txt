##Recipe
2 chicken breasts, cubed
2 cups cooked rice
2 eggs
1.5 bag frozen peas and carrots
.5 bag frozen corn
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1 onion
4 teaspoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons sesame oil
1 box egg rolls or pot stickers


- Cook egg rolls according to package directions.
- Heat oil in pan; cook garlic, onion, and chicken breasts.  
- Add frozen vegetables and heat through.  
- Crack eggs into chicken and veggie mix - stir to coat and cook through.  
- Add rice and soy sauce; mix well.
