## Recipe
INGREDIENTS

2 cup white rice
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced
2 heads broccoli, cut in small pieces
4 teaspoons curry powder
1 cup plain yogurt
1 cup heavy cream (half pint container)
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 14.5-ounce cans diced tomatoes
meat from 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
DIRECTIONS

Cook the rice according to the package directions.
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-low heat. Add the onion and broccoli, cook stirring occasionally, for 7 minutes.
Sprinkle with the curry powder and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
Add the yogurt and cream and simmer gently for 3 minutes. Stir in the salt, pepper, tomatoes, and chicken and heat through.  Serve over rice.
