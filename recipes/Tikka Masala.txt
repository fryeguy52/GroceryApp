##recipe
1 can crushed tomatoes
1 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons tomato paste
2 teaspoons garam masala
1.5 pounds chicken thighs
1 cucumber, halved and thinly sliced
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
2 cups rice
.5 cup heavy cream
naan


- Combine tomatoes, onion, garlic, tomato paste, garam masala, salt, and pepper in bottom of crockpot.  Place chicken on to, cover, and cook on low 7-8 hours.
- In small bowl, toss the cucumber with lemon juice and salt and pepper.  Refrigerate up to 8 hours.
- Cook rice.
- Heat naan according to package directions.
- Add cream to chicken just before sering.
