##recipe
1 pound chicken breasts, diced
3 T olive oil
0.5 yellow onion, diced
1 cup uncooked rice
1 can fire roasted diced tomatoes, drained
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 t garlic powder
1 t onion powder
0.5 t paprika
2 t chili powder
1 t cumin
2.25 c chicken broth
2 c shredded cheese
1 tomato, diced
4 green onions, sliced
2 avocados, diced or made into guacamole


- Add 1 T oil to IP, hit Saute button, adjust to set to "More".
- Season chicken with 1 t salt and 0.5 t pepper.
- Once oil is hot, add chicken to pot.
- When chicken is browned, add onions until they start to soften.
- Add 1 T olive oil and uncooked rice; toast for 2 min.
- Stir in black beans, canned tomatoes, chicken broth, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, paprika, and cumin.
- Lock IP and set to manual, high pressure for 8 minutes.
- Use Quick Pressure Release, open IP and fluff with fork.
- Sprinkle with cheese, recover, and let set for 2-3 min to melt cheese.
- Garnish with tomatoes, green onions, and avocado.
