##recipe
Ingredients
============
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 yellow onion , diced
- 5 carrots , diced
- 5 stalks celery , diced
- 1 teaspoon Kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
- 3 cloves garlic , minced
- 3 cups ham , diced
- 45 ounces white Cannellini beans , drained and rinsed
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 cups chicken broth

Instructions
=============
1. Add the butter to a large dutch oven over medium heat with your onion, carrots, celery, salt and pepper.
1. Cook for 6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until onions are translucent.
1. Add in the garlic and stir well, cooking for 1 minute.
1. Add in the ham, cannelini beans, thyme, rosemary, bay leaves and chicken broth and stir well.
1. On medium-high heat, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 30 minutes.
