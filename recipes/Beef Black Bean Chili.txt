##recipe
1 lb of ground beef
28 oz can of tomato sauce
2 cans of black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can of petite diced tomatoes, undrained
1 2/3 c frozen corn
2 large cloves of garlic, minced
1 T paprika
1 T chili powder
2 t ground cumin
1 1/2 t ground oregano
1/4 t crushed red pepper flakes
1 bag shredded cheese
2 boxes corn bread mix


- Put everything but corn bread in crockpot.  Cook on low for 8-10 hours.  
- Make cornbread according to package directions, adding an extra egg and 1/4 c sour cream to mix.
- Serve with shredded cheese
