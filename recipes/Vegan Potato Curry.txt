##recipe
3 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 Onion (Chopped)
1 Tbsp Crushed Garlic
1 Tbsp Minced Ginger
4 tsp Garam Masala (or Curry Powder)
1 tsp Paprika
1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1 tsp Cumin
1/2 tsp Coriander Powder
1 tsp Turmeric
2 bags baby carrots
3 15oz (425g) Can Chickpeas (Drained)
1 14oz (400g) Can Chopped Tomatoes
1 cup (240ml) Vegetable Stock
1 14oz (400ml) Can Coconut Milk (Full Fat)
1 box frozen spinach
2 Tbsp Coconut Sugar (or Brown Sugar)
Sea Salt and Black Pepper (To Taste)
1/2 cup Cilantro (Chopped, For Serving)

INSTRUCTIONS
Thaw spinach.
Add the olive oil to a pot with the chopped onion, crushed garlic, minced ginger, garam masala, paprika, cayenne pepper, cumin, coriander powder and turmeric and saut√© until the onions are slightly softened.
Add the carrots and chickpeas and toss in the spices until well mixed.
Add the chopped tomatoes, vegetable stock and coconut milk and stir in.
Bring to the boil and then reduce heat, cover the pot and simmer until the carrots are cooked. Add the thawed spinach and heat for 1 minute.
Add coconut sugar, sea salt and black pepper (to taste).
Serve with rice and/or vegan naan bread.
