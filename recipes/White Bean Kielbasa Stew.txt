##recipe
1 pound dried white beans
1 turkey kielbasa, halved and sliced .5" thick
4 cups chicken broth
1 can diced tomatoes
1 onion, chopped
6 cloves garlic minched
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
5 ounces baby spinach
1 loaf country bread

Combine beans, kielbasa, broth, tomatoes, onion, garlic, rosemary, and 1 cup water.  Cook on low for 8 hours.  Stir in spinach just before serving.  Serve with bread.
