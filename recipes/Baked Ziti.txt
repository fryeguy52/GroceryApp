##Recipe
1 box penne
1 pound ground beef
1 onion
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 jar spaghetti sauce
16 ounces ricotta
2 teaspoons italian seasoning
1 package frozen spinach, thawed
4 ounces parmesan
8 ounces mozzarella
1 package salad mix
1 tomato
1 cucumber


- Cook pasta. 
- Brown garlic, onion, and beef.  
- Mix together pasta, beef mixture, sauce, spices, spinach, and parmesan.  Place in oiled 9x13 baking dish.  Sprinkle mozzarella on top.  - Cover with foil and bake at 375 for ~40 minutes.  Remove foil and bake 5 minutes until cheese is browned.  Serve with side salad.
