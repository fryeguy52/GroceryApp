## recipe
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
1 can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can hominy, rinsed and drained
1 can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
1 bag green chile
3 small zucchini, shredded
2 pounds ground turkey
1 medium onion, chopped
1-1/2 to 2 cups water
1 to 2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
4 cloves garlic, minced
0.5 teaspoon sugar
2 boxes cornbread mix
3 eggs
0.66 c milk
0.25 c greek yogurt or sour cream
0.5 cup shredded Monterey Jack 



- Make cornbread according to package directions, adding an extra egg and 0.25 cup greek yogurt to the recipe on box.
- In a Dutch oven, sauté onions until brown.  Add garlic and turkey; sauté until browned.  
- Add spices and sauté for a few minutes.  Add tomato sauce and paste and scrape bottom of pan.  
- Add beans, hominy, and zucchini. Bring to a boil.
- Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 minutes. 
- Garnish chili with cheese.
