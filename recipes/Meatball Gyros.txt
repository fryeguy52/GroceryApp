##recipe
Ingredients
===========

- 1 package (12 oz.) Turkey Meatballs
- 1 package gyro meat
- 4 whole wheat flatbreads or pitas
- 0.5 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tomato, diced
- 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
- 1 container Tzatziki sauce
- 0.5 jar kalamata olives
- 1 green pepper, diced
- 1 container, feta
- 1 bottle Dion's Greek


Salad
============

1. Mix chopped veggies with dressing.

Gyros
============

1. Cook meatballs in microwave.
1. Cook gyro meat in a large skillet on medium high heat. 
1. Heat flatbread in oven for 4 min.
1. Assemble and serve gyros with salad.
