## recipe
2 tablespoons of butter
1 lb of shrimp, thawed
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 lemon, freshly squeezed
0.5 cup of chicken broth or water
2 tablespoons of fresh parsley
freshly grated parmesan cheese
8 oz of angel hair pasta
salt and pepper to taste
salad mix
tomato
cucumber


- In a large pot of boiling water cook angel hair pasta according to directions.
- Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat.
- Add shrimp and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally until the shrimp turns pink. About 2-4 minutes.
- Stir in lemon juice and chicken broth (you can use water). Bring to a simmer.
- Stir in fresh parsley and parmesan cheese.
- Toss with angel hair pasta and garnish with more parmesan and parsley. 
- Serve with side salad.
